About the Show

A platform built for the Caribbean voice

Caribbean Chronicles by JOS is a storytelling platform dedicated to showcasing the depth, brilliance, and personality of the Caribbean. Through long-form conversations, cultural analysis, and bite-sized segments, we shine a light on the region's innovators, artists, and change-makers.

From fintech disruption in the Eastern Caribbean to the diaspora's global influence on tech and culture — this is the Caribbean conversation that the world needs to hear.

Hosted by JOS, a storyteller and cultural curator who blends humor, insight, and lived experience to spotlight the Caribbean beyond clichés.

Caribbean Chronicles by JOS is produced by JOS Productions™ and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or partnered with any other organizations.

JOS Productions

A Caribbean media tech startup

JOS Productions is an early-stage Caribbean media technology company building digital platforms that amplify Caribbean voices, culture, and innovation for a global audience. We develop podcast infrastructure, newsletter distribution systems, and digital content pipelines purpose-built for the Caribbean and its diaspora.

Our flagship product, Caribbean Chronicles by JOS, serves as both a content brand and proof-of-concept for scalable Caribbean digital media — demonstrating real market demand for high-quality, tech-forward Caribbean storytelling.

Our technology roadmap includes audience analytics tools for Caribbean creators, a content discovery platform for Caribbean media, and AI-assisted content production workflows to scale editorial output across podcast, newsletter, and social channels.
